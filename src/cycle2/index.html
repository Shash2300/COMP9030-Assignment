Our Mission

The Indigenous Art Atlas is dedicated to documenting, preserving, and celebrating indigenous art across Australia. We create a space where communities can share appropriate cultural knowledge while protecting sacred and sensitive sites.

Our platform prioritizes cultural sensitivity, community input, and respectful engagement with indigenous art and heritage.

Acknowledgment of Country

We acknowledge the Traditional Owners of Country throughout Australia and recognize their continuing connection to land, waters, and culture. We pay our respects to their Elders past, present, and emerging, and extend that respect to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.
