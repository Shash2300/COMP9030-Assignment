Usage Guidelines

Submission Guidelines

What to Submit

  • Indigenous art from any period (ancient to contemporary)
  • Art in various forms: cave art, murals, sculptures, installations, gallery pieces
  • Clear, high-quality photos
  • Accurate descriptions and cultural context

What NOT to Submit

  • Sacred or restricted ceremony imagery
  • Content depicting deceased persons (without permission)
  • Exact locations of burial sites or sacred sites
  • Secret/sacred knowledge not meant for public sharing
  • Culturally appropriated or misrepresented art

Cultural Sensitivity

When submitting art entries:

  • Mark culturally sensitive locations appropriately
  • Obtain permission for contemporary art
  • Provide proper attribution to artists and communities
  • Include appropriate cultural context
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property

Community Standards

All users must:

  • Treat indigenous culture with respect
  • Provide accurate information
  • Report inappropriate content
  • Follow submission guidelines
