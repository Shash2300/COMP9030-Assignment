Ethical Considerations

Cultural Sensitivity

This platform is built on principles of respect for indigenous cultures, traditional knowledge, and sacred sites. We acknowledge that indigenous art is not merely aesthetic objects but carries deep cultural, spiritual, and historical significance.

Location Privacy

We implement a three-tier location privacy system:

  • Exact Location: For public art and galleries
  • General Area: For sites that should not be heavily trafficked
  • Hidden Location: For sacred and restricted sites

Sacred Knowledge Protection

We take special care to:

  • Prevent publication of sacred or restricted imagery
  • Protect exact locations of burial sites and ceremony grounds
  • Review all submissions before public display
  • Respond quickly to community concerns

Artist Recognition

We are committed to:

  • Proper attribution of all artwork
  • Supporting indigenous artists
  • Fighting cultural appropriation
  • Providing platforms for artist profiles

Non-Commercial Commitment

This platform is non-commercial and dedicated to cultural preservation and education, not profit.

Acknowledgment of Country

We acknowledge the Traditional Owners of Country throughout Australia and recognize their continuing connection to land, waters, and culture. We pay our respects to their Elders past, present, and emerging.
