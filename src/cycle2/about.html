About Indigenous Art Atlas

Our Mission

The Indigenous Art Atlas is a community-driven platform dedicated to documenting, preserving, and celebrating indigenous art across Australia. We create a space where communities can share appropriate cultural knowledge while protecting sacred and sensitive sites.

Our Values

  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respecting indigenous knowledge and sacred sites
  • Community Empowerment: Giving voice to indigenous artists and communities
  • Education: Fostering understanding and appreciation of indigenous culture
  • Preservation: Documenting art for future generations

How It Works

  1. Discover: Browse our interactive map and collection of indigenous art
  2. Submit: Register and submit art entries with photos and descriptions
  3. Review: All submissions are reviewed by moderators for cultural sensitivity
  4. Share: Approved entries become part of our community collection

Contact Us

Acknowledgment of Country

We acknowledge the Traditional Owners of Country throughout Australia and recognize their continuing connection to land, waters, and culture.
